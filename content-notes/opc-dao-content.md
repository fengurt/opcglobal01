# OPC DAO Content Summary

## Project Aletheia (真理计划)
OPC DAO's core strategy for creating a blockchain-based "clean knowledge base"

## Three-Layer Architecture

### 1. Technology Layer (Immutability)
- **Arweave + Irys**: Permanent data storage
- **WeaveDB**: High-frequency database logic
- **Glacier Network**: AI-readable vector indexing

### 2. Governance Layer (Open Source Security)
- **Optimism Citizens' House**: Reputation-based governance
- **Gitcoin Passport**: Anti-Sybil identity verification
- **Kleros**: Decentralized arbitration court

### 3. Economic Layer (Sovereign Fund)
- **Cayman Foundation Company**: Legal entity
- **Asset Lock**: "Never distribute dividends"
- **RWA Investment**: S&P 500 and MSCI Global Index through Backed Finance and Ondo Finance

## Key Principles
1. Data Sovereignty & Censorship Resistance
2. Anti-Sybil & Source Traceability
3. Non-Profit Pure Motivation
4. Open Source Code, Governance, and Algorithms

## Spending Policy
- 4% Golden Rule (Yale/Princeton model)
- Smoothing Rule: Annual Spending = (70% × Previous Year) + (30% × 4% × Current Fund Value)
